Types of Florida Traffic School Courses Available in 2025

Different infractions or requirements will demand different course types. Make sure the school you choose offers the course you need:

4-Hour Basic Driver Improvement (BDI)

Most commonly used for:

  • Moving violations

  • Preventing insurance rate increases

  • Keeping points off your record

8-Hour Intermediate Driver Improvement (IDI)

Required if you've been ordered by a judge or have committed multiple violations within a short time.

12-Hour Advanced Driver Improvement (ADI)

Used for:

  • License reinstatement

  • Habitual traffic offenders

  • Serious driving violations
