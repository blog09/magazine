How to Reset the Reolink Camera?

If you are looking for a security camera for your home or office, consider the Reolink camera. The camera has interesting safety features and gives the users amazing functioning. If you have installed the camera, you may be well aware of its functioning.

However, many people have faced the issue of resetting the camera due to some reasons. Several reasons can arise when there is a need to reset it. To solve the issues, you should know some simple steps.

Why do You need To Reset Your Reolink Camera?

Every electronic device has to be reset timely for better work. The Reolink camera may also need the same due to several reasons which you should know. When you know the reasons, only then can you solve the issues and execute the resetting process. The reasons may include the following:

  • If there is an issue with the proper functioning of the camera.

  • You could create a new password by resetting it if you forgot it.

  • If you give away the camera, resetting will clear all your details.

How to Reset Reolink Camera through App?

If you are using the Reolink camera through an application on the phone and now need to reset it, you can do that easily. You need to follow some simple instructions for resetting the camera to defaults, and this may include:

  • Start the application

  • Go to camera settings

  • Click on the button "reset camera."

  • Next, fill in your password and click on "ok" to confirm

  • Once you have considered these simple steps, you can reset your camera to the default settings.

How to Reset Reolink Camera through Reolink Client?

If you want to reset the camera through the Reolink client, you can also easily do it by following simple steps. Start by launching the Reolink client and logging in to the camera. Choose the camera device from the list you want to factory reset.

Then go to advanced settings and, scroll down, select factory reset. You need to click on confirm, which can lead to new settings.

How to Reset Reolink Camera through Web Browser?

Resetting your camera will lead it to the original state when it was in the beginning. The resetting steps will be useful when you want to sell off your camera and need to remove personal information from it. To reset the Reolink camera through the web browser, you can choose to follow simple steps, which include:

  • First, launch the web browser and go to the camera's IP address.

  • Go to the system settings, click maintenance, and go to factory reset.

  • Finally, confirm, and you will be able to reset the camera.
