Veeva Vault is a cloud-native platform specifically designed for regulated industries, primarily life sciences (pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and medical devices). It serves as a unified system to manage both unstructured content (documents, images, videos) and structured data in a single location. 

Core Components & Purpose

  • Unified Source of Truth: It eliminates data silos by connecting processes across research and development (R&D), quality, regulatory, safety, and commercial functions.
  • Cloud Architecture: Built as a multi-tenant Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform, it is accessible via web browsers and mobile devices.
  • Compliance-Ready: Designed to meet rigorous global standards, including FDA 21 CFR Part 11 and EU Annex 11, with built-in audit trails, electronic signatures, and version control. 

Key Application Suites (2026)

The platform hosts over 50 specialized applications categorized into distinct "Clouds":

  • Clinical: Tools for trial management, such as electronic Trial Master Files (eTMF), Clinical Trial Management Systems (CTMS), and Clinical Data Management Suites (CDMS).
  • Quality: Manages quality processes (QMS) like CAPA, deviations, and audits, along with GxP document control (QualityDocs).
  • Regulatory: Known as Vault RIM, it unifies product registrations, submissions, and archival of regulatory filings.
  • Safety: Handles pharmacovigilance and adverse event reporting through Vault Safety.
  • Commercial: Includes PromoMats for promotional content management and the next-generation Vault CRM, which recently migrated to the Vault platform from Salesforce. 

Recent Innovations (2025–2026)

Veeva AI: Integrated "agentic AI" features that provide industry-specific assistants for tasks like document summarization, compliance checks, and medical coding.

Vault CRM Migration: By early 2026, Veeva has seen rapid adoption of its next-gen CRM built natively on the Vault platform, moving away from its legacy Salesforce-based architecture.

Vault Basics: A pre-configured, turnkey solution launched for emerging biotechs to deploy core clinical, quality, and regulatory tools in as little as 30–60 days.
